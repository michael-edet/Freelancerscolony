Scaling Your Freelance Business

May 28, 2023 45 min 1.2K downloads

Episode Summary

In this episode, we discuss proven strategies to scale your freelance business beyond the solo practitioner model. Learn how to:

  • Transition from freelancer to agency owner
  • Build systems that allow your business to grow
  • Hire and manage subcontractors effectively
  • Create recurring revenue streams

Episode Highlights

  • 04:20 - The mindset shift needed for scaling
  • 12:45 - When to consider hiring help
  • 22:10 - Pricing models for scaled services
  • 35:30 - Tools for managing a growing team
